About Us

JapanSSW is your one-stop platform for skilled trade job seekers under the Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) visa in Japan.

Founded in 2024 by Juan Angelo, JapanSSW provides job matching, visa guidance, and community resources to help foreign workers find stable employment and build better futures in Japan.
